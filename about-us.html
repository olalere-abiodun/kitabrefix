WHO WE ARE

K.I-T.A.B BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT SERVICE LIMITED is a business solutions firm committed to simplifying and accelerating the process of Business Setup, Consulting, Marketing & Advertising, Tax Registration and Real Estate. We address the critical pain points that entrepreneurs, startups, and growing enterprises face when navigating complex regulatory frameworks, bureaucratic processes, and competitive markets.

Our all-in-one service hub enables clients to; Register and structure their businesses, secure tax identification numbers and compliance documentation, design and execute strategic marketing plans, and access verified listings of government-owned properties for lease or purchase.

By leveraging strong local partnerships, in-depth regulatory expertise, and cutting-edge digital tools. K.I-T.A.B streamlines traditionally cumbersome processes into a seamless,efficient and result-driven experience empowering you to thrive.

VISION & MISSION

Our mission is to empower businesses to launch and scale faster in Lagos and other states in Nigeria by removing administrative burdens and unlocking access to valuable government assets and services. With our expert guidance and tailored solutions, we help clients move from idea to operation with confidence, speed, and compliance

To be the leading catalyst for business growth in Lagos and other states in Nigeria by simplifying access to government services, regulatory compliance, and strategic market opportunities.

OUR PROCESS

K.I-T.A.B BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT SERVICES LIMITED started in 2018 as K.I-T.A.B BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT SERVICES (Enterprise). We have become a fully registered limited company, driven by a strong and diverse client portfolio and a reputation for excellence.

We are a dynamic and innovative company dedicated to delivering comprehensive solutions across an array of key areas; Business Setup, Marketing & Advertising, Taxes & Regulatory Compliance, & Real Estate. We accelerate complex government processes and regulatory requirements, helping our clients establish themselves in a new business environment.

Our customer-centric approach ensures that we consistently exceed expectations and deliver tangible results for clients worldwide. At K.I-T.A.B, we go beyond consulting. we represent you in your absence.

OUR CLIENT BASE

  • Startups & Entrepreneurs seeking fast, affordable business registration and go-to-market support.
  • SMEs needing ongoing compliance, marketing, and expansion services.
  • Real Estate Investors & Developers interested in acquiring or leasing government-owned assets.
  • Foreign Investors entering the Nigerian market who require end-to-end setup, advisory, and operational support.
  • Nonprofits & Cooperatives needing formal registration and compliance guidance.

This diverse client base shares a common need: streamlined access to critical services that reduce friction, save time, and unlock new opportunities in the vibrant but complex Lagos and Nigerian business environment.

OUR FOCUS

Our core areas of service include:

  • Business registration and regulatory compliance
  • Tax identification, VAT, and CAC documentation
  • Strategic brand development and marketing support
  • Government property acquisition and lease facilitation
  • Local partnership facilitation and advisory services
