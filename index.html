About Us

K.I-T.A.B Service Limited is a solutions-driven firm dedicated to simplifying and accelerating business growth in Nigeria. We help entrepreneurs, startups, and enterprises navigate challenges such as regulatory compliance, tax registration, marketing, and real estate opportunities.

Our mission is to empower businesses to launch and scale with confidence by removing administrative burdens and unlocking access to valuable government services and assets. With strong local partnerships, deep regulatory expertise, and modern digital tools, we transform complex processes into seamless, result-driven experiences.

At K.I-T.A.B, we don’t just consult — we represent you, ensuring your business moves from idea to operation with speed, efficiency, and compliance.
